Privacy Policy

Tone & Fit is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's how we handle your data.

Effective Date: February 8, 2026

Information Collection

Tone & Fit does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

When you use the app, you may take or upload a photo for color analysis. These images are transmitted securely to a third-party AI service solely for the purpose of analyzing your skin tone and providing personalized color recommendations.

Data Usage & Storage

Images you provide are used exclusively for extracting color analysis data. They are not stored on our servers, linked to any user identity, or used for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.

Analysis results are stored locally on your device so you can access them later. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

AI processing service — for analyzing your photo and generating personalized color recommendations.
RevenueCat — for managing in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. See their privacy policy for details.
Apple App Store — for processing payments. Apple's standard terms apply.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties.

Children's Privacy

Tone & Fit does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
